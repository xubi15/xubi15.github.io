ForestGump Walking Tracker Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2024/01/18

Welcome to ForestGump Walking Tracker! This Privacy Policy outlines the information we collect, how we use it, and your choices about our practices. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and other account-related information.
  • Health and Fitness Data: ForestGump Walking Tracker tracks your daily steps, distance, calories burned, and other fitness-related information.

Usage Information

  • App Usage: We collect data on how you use the ForestGump Walking Tracker app, such as the features you interact with, and the frequency and duration of your use.

Environmental Contributions

  • Eco-Friendly Actions: ForestGump converts your steps into eco-friendly actions, and we collect information related to your participation in environmental initiatives.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • Personalization: Customizing your experience with personalized insights, achievements, and notifications.
  • Health and Fitness: Providing you with health insights and helping you achieve your fitness goals.
  • Environmental Impact: Demonstrating the positive impact of your daily activity on the environment.

Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for their direct marketing purposes. However, we may share information as follows:

  • With Your Consent: We may share your information with your consent or as directed by you.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or government requests.

Privacy and Security

  • Data Security: Your data is protected with advanced encryption protocols.
  • Privacy Settings: You have control over what information you share. Customize your privacy settings based on your preferences.

Your Choices

  • Account Information: You can review and update your account information through the ForestGump Walking Tracker app.
  • Privacy Settings: Adjust your privacy settings to control the information you share.

Updates to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. Check the date at the top to see when it was last updated.
